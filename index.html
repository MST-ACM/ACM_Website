What to Expect from ACM

We have a variety of Special Interest Groups (SIG's) and events.

ACM-Women

The Missouri S&T ACM-W chapter aims to facilitate a thriving community of women in computing.

SIG-Competition

SIG-Comp is a group that focuses on programming competitions and algorithmic theory. Great for learning.

SIG-Security

SIG-Sec teaches and practices network security, hacking, and cryptography.

SIG-Game

SIG-Game builds and hosts an AI tournament and throughout the year, develops platforms to facilitate it.

SIG.com

SIG.com is the web division of ACM, creators of this website, and the infrastructure behind a smarter ACM.

SIG-Lounge

SIG-Lounge is in charge of the lounge in the Computer Science building at Missouri S&T.

SIG-Tutor

SIG-Tutor valiantly fights to tackle bad grades by organizing study sessions and groups for CS education.
